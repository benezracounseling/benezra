About Me

I am here to hold space for you to share whatever it is that is weighing heavy on you, and offer the support you need to inevitably release it. I believe that what most people want is to be seen for the pure soul that they came to this Earth as, and what they need help with is reprogramming some of the unconscious beliefs that society has instilled in them.

Some of the ways in which I help my clients is by utilizing narrative, cognitive, and existential therapy to bring compassion to what lives in the unseen. Through formal training and deep intuition, I've become a heart-led therapist who offers a safe, supportive space for healing.

Rooted in spiritual, narrative, and cognitive behavioral therapy, my approach blends structure and soul to help you feel truly seen as we gently explore your path forward. I am happy to adapt to your wants and needs. I would be honored if you were to trust me with your healing journey. I understand that life can be hard sometimes and I want to assure you that you do not have to go through it alone. I am here to offer you support in any way that I can and would love for you to reach out if you resonate with my profile.

My Therapeutic Approach

Narrative Therapy

Helping you separate from problematic stories and rewrite your life narrative with empowerment and hope.

Cognitive Behavioral

Identifying and transforming unhelpful thought patterns that influence emotions and behaviors.

Existential Therapy

Exploring life's deeper questions to find meaning, purpose, and authentic self-expression.
